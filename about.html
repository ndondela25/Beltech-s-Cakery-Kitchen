About Us

Beltech’s Cakery is a sweet meat company in Lusaka and Shibuyunji, which produces and sells high-end baked goods to retail and wholesale customers. We sell pre-made as well as made-to-order confections using locally available ingredients. The business also offers cake decorating classes to customers as part of its services.

Our Vision

At Beltech’s Cakery & Kitchen we visualize a business that can hold its own in a very competitive market, by optimizing all available resources in an honest, ethical way to ensure customer satisfaction through excellent service and excellent products whilst contributing to the community.

Our Mission

To ensure client satisfaction and loyalty as we deliver personal and unique experiences that are beyond expectation and create long term loyal relationships by consistently providing outstanding service that creates an overall extraordinary cake Experience.
